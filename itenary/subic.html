Visit Subic for fun filled trip

Summer is coming to it's end, and you haven't made the most of it yet?Come to SUBIC! With just 2 and half hours drive away from manila, you will discover and experience many wonderful places and activities that make your summer memorable.

There are so many tourist spots to choose from, so it might get a bit overwhelming to choose where to go. Besides its beautiful beaches and delicious restaurants, the area also offers a lot of different Subic activities. From having picnics by the El Kabayo Falls to hiking the Pamulaklakin Forest Trail, Subic is sure to make the rest of your year!
